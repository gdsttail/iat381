About Us

GD (Green Dale) is a young travel agent built up by Colin Richer and Neo Smith in 2012. The official website was launched in Novermber 2012 to create unique online explore and purchase experience about Vancouver local events, restaurants, and entertainments. We came up with the idea for GD as a global promotion for our city. There are so many things to do in Great Vancouver, you can get a clue about the activities in this city, and create a fantastic trip for you and your family or friends with our website. Through our site, you will get to see the beautiful life in Vancouver, as well as purchase the discounted event coupons directly. This is what our company stands for and how we want to help your travels. Get in touch with us see the world through our eyes.
